About Us

Asaan Technologies is an agri-fintech startup which aims to disrupt finance and food industry like never before.

  • On the fintech part, Asaan aim to democratize investing, and bring financial security, financial inclusion, and financial independence to masses.
  • On agritech part, Asaan aim to provide food security to the nations, innovation and sustainability to rural economy.
